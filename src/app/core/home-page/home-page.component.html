about Us..

Small farms is an Agricultural solutions firm that provides a wide range of digital products and services for small and subsistence farmers. Our objective is to give farmers and their customers access to digital tools that will enable them to :

  1. Get information on optimal agricultural practices
  2. Access digital platforms for recording, tracking and reporting their financial, human resources, agricultural, procurement, stocking and project management activities
  3. Provide transparency to consumers on how food is produced
